System 5: Revenue Analytics &
Sales Commission Dashboard

Browser-based analytics tool enabling the accounting team to segment revenue by service type and track new customer acquisition linked to individual sales representatives.

JavaScript HTML/CSS QuickBooks Online Excel Online
Business Problem

Why This Tool Was Built

Revenue data at KDExpress Canada was previously fragmented across multiple spreadsheets with no structured segmentation by service type. Management had limited visibility into which service lines were performing, and commission tracking for sales representatives was inconsistent and time-consuming.

  • No structured reporting by service type — revenue trends were unclear
  • New customer attribution was manual — commission disputes arose frequently
  • Resource allocation decisions were made without reliable data
Solution Developed

Key Capabilities

Revenue segmentation
Automated breakdown of revenue by service type — bi-weekly reporting cycle
Customer attribution
Systematic identification of new customers linked to individual sales representatives
Commission tracking
Supports accurate and transparent commission calculation — reduces disputes
Management insights
Data-driven resource allocation — identify high-performing service lines
