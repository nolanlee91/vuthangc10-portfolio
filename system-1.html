Business Problem

Why This System Was Built

KDExpress Canada operates a parcel consolidation and international forwarding service across multiple Canadian warehouse locations. Prior to this system, operations relied on a legacy barcode-based system that recorded only tracking numbers — without customer mapping, shipment status visibility, or operational coordination tools.

  • Customer Service staff spent approximately 2–3 hours per day manually cross-referencing parcels across Zalo group chats to identify ownership
  • Warehouse staff lacked real-time visibility into shipment completeness across locations
  • Customers had no direct access to shipment information — all enquiries required CS intervention
Solution Developed

System Capabilities

I independently designed and developed a full-stack logistics management platform to replace the legacy system and integrate operational workflows across all departments.
