Business Problem

Why This System Was Built

KDExpress Canada operates a fulfilment service collecting payments on behalf of customers. Each payment generates a notification email containing transaction details. Prior to this system, all reconciliation was performed manually.

  • Accounting staff reviewed each payment notification email individually and recorded separate journal entries in QuickBooks Online
  • With hundreds of daily transactions, this consumed approximately 2 hours per accounting staff member per day
  • Manual entry resulted in an estimated 10–15% error rate — reconciliation discrepancies and bank matching issues
  • Customer attribution was entirely manual — staff had to identify which payment corresponded to which customer
Solution Developed

System Capabilities

I independently designed and implemented an automated financial workflow using Microsoft Power Automate, integrated with QuickBooks Online and Excel Online. I acted as a bridge between logistics operations and financial accounting — designing a data flow where operational payment triggers are automatically reflected in financial records without delay or manual intervention.

Automated email trigger
System continuously monitors the payment inbox and processes each incoming transaction in real time
Structured data extraction
