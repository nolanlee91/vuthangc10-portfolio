Business Problem

Why This System Was Built

Warehouse staff across all KDExpress Canada locations relied on handheld barcode scanners that captured only tracking numbers at the point of parcel intake. Critical shipment data — customer reference, recipient name, weight, and receipt date — was not recorded at intake.

  • All additional shipment data had to be manually entered after intake — inconsistent processes and frequent data gaps
  • Warehouse staff spent approximately 2–3 hours per day on manual data entry, with high risk of input errors and parcel misidentification
  • Hundreds of parcels arriving daily across multiple locations amplified the impact of these inefficiencies
Solution Developed

System Capabilities

I independently designed and developed a mobile-optimised AI-powered intake system that eliminates manual data entry at the point of receipt.

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash OCR
Warehouse staff capture parcel label images via mobile — system automatically extracts tracking number, customer reference, recipient name, weight, and receipt date
Prompt engineering
Interprets diverse label formats from international and domestic carriers, converting unstructured visual data into standardised database records
Real-time sync
Extracted data synchronised instantly with System 1 — no duplicate entry
Image audit trail
Each parcel stored with its label image via Cloudinary — verifiable documentation at point of receipt
Automated customer mapping
System intelligently assigns parcels to customer accounts even when multiple recipient identities are used
