System 6: Multi-Service Shipping Rate
Calculator & Invoice Generator

Web-based pricing and invoicing tool consolidating all service rates into a single platform with customer self-service and automated invoice generation.

React.js Node.js QuickBooks Online Netlify
Business Problem

Why This Tool Was Built

Pricing at KDExpress Canada was managed across multiple Google Sheets with different rate tables for different service types. Staff had to manually calculate quotes and create invoices individually in QuickBooks — time-consuming and prone to inconsistency.

  • Multiple Google Sheets for different services — inconsistent rates and frequent errors
  • All quotes required CS staff involvement — no customer self-service
  • Invoices created manually per transaction — slow billing cycle
Solution Developed

Key Capabilities

Unified pricing engine
All service types and customer tiers consolidated in a single platform — eliminates spreadsheet fragmentation
Customer self-service
Shareable web link — customers calculate shipping costs independently without contacting CS
Auto invoice generation
Invoice automatically generated upon pricing completion — accelerates billing cycle
Rate management
Simplified rate updates through direct data input — no spreadsheet management required
